Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Safety Empire, the Council of 12 AIs, and JABL tokens.

General Questions

What is AI Safety Empire?

AI Safety Empire is the world's first democratic AI governance platform powered by the Council of 12 AIs. We provide tools, APIs, and services to make AI safer, more transparent, and accountable through collective intelligence and blockchain verification.

What is the Council of 12 AIs?

The Council of 12 AIs is a democratic governance system consisting of 12 specialized AI members from OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), and Google (Gemini). Each AI has a specific role (security, ethics, safety, etc.) and votes on decisions democratically. A supermajority of 83% is required for approval, with Jabulon (The Lawgiver) holding veto power.

How does the Council make decisions?

When a decision is submitted, all 12 AI members analyze it from their specialized perspectives. They vote democratically, requiring at least 10 out of 12 votes (83%) to approve. Jabulon, The Lawgiver, can veto any decision. All votes are recorded on the blockchain for transparency and immutability.

Who created AI Safety Empire?

AI Safety Empire was founded by a team of AI safety researchers, blockchain developers, and ethical AI advocates committed to creating democratic governance systems for artificial intelligence.

JABL Tokens & Cryptocurrency

What are JABL tokens?

JABL (JabulonCoin) is our native ERC-20 cryptocurrency token with a fixed supply of 1 billion tokens. JABL powers the entire AI Safety Empire ecosystem, allowing users to pay for services, earn rewards, stake for additional benefits, and participate in governance.

How do I earn JABL tokens?

You can earn JABL tokens by:

  • Using the platform (all plans include monthly JABL rewards)
  • Contributing to the Council of 12 AIs
  • Running deepfake detection scans
  • Reporting AI safety issues
  • Participating in governance voting
  • Staking JABL to earn AEGIS tokens

Can I use JABL tokens to pay for subscriptions?

Yes! JABL tokens can be used as an alternative payment method for all subscription tiers. For example, instead of paying $49/month for Professional, you can pay with 10,000 JABL tokens. This often provides better value as token prices may be lower than fiat currency equivalents.

Where can I buy or sell JABL tokens?

JABL tokens can be purchased directly through our platform or traded on decentralized exchanges (DEX) like Uniswap. Once deployed to mainnet, JABL will also be available on major cryptocurrency exchanges.

What is staking and how does it work?

Staking allows you to lock up your JABL tokens to earn rewards in the form of AEGIS tokens. When you stake JABL, you help secure the network and participate in governance. In return, you receive a percentage of transaction fees and newly minted AEGIS tokens. Staking rewards vary based on the amount staked and duration.

What are AEGIS tokens?

AEGIS tokens are premium governance tokens earned by staking JABL. AEGIS holders have enhanced voting rights in platform governance and access to exclusive features. AEGIS tokens represent long-term commitment to the AI Safety Empire ecosystem.

Pricing & Billing

Is there a free plan?

Yes! Our Free tier includes 100 API calls per month, 1 Council decision per day, 10 JABL tokens monthly, and community support. It's perfect for trying out the platform and small-scale projects.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Technical Questions

What APIs do you provide?

We provide comprehensive REST APIs for:

  • Council of 12 AIs voting and decision-making
  • Deepfake detection and content verification
  • AI security monitoring and threat detection
  • Bias detection and fairness analysis
  • Transparency and explainability tools
  • JABL token management and staking

Do you provide SDKs?

Yes! We provide official SDKs for Python and JavaScript/TypeScript. Both SDKs include full documentation, code examples, and type definitions. Professional tier and above includes SDK access.

What blockchain do you use?

We use Ethereum-compatible blockchains (Polygon, Base) for smart contracts and decision logging. This ensures transparency, immutability, and low transaction costs while maintaining compatibility with the broader Ethereum ecosystem.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All Council decisions are logged on blockchain for transparency, but sensitive user data remains private and encrypted. We are SOC 2 compliant and follow GDPR regulations.

What is your API uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all paid plans. Enterprise customers receive a 99.99% SLA with financial penalties for downtime. Our infrastructure is distributed across multiple regions with automatic failover.

Can I integrate AI Safety Empire with my existing systems?

Yes! Our RESTful APIs and webhooks make integration straightforward. We also provide custom integration support for Business and Enterprise customers. Common integrations include CI/CD pipelines, content management systems, and AI development platforms.
